Introduction

Rajalakshmi Institute Of Technology (RIT) is renowned for its excellence in education and placement opportunities. Our institute provides comprehensive infrastructure and support for training and placement activities. With a focus on holistic development, we prepare our students to excel in their professional careers.

Training and Placement Cell

The Training and Placement Cell at RIT plays a vital role in enhancing the employability of our students. We offer specialized training in various areas such as mathematical ability, aptitude skills, analytical skills, language skills, and technical skills. Professional trainers from both in-house resources and corporate sectors conduct these training sessions.

Our flagship event, the Residential Employability Training Program, is a premier initiative aimed at preparing students to face the challenges of the corporate world. This program is offered free of cost to all our students, and it is eagerly anticipated by them.

Headed by a Placement Officer, the Training and Placement Cell comprises faculty members from each department, along with student coordinators. Our primary objective is to provide personal and career-related support to our students, ultimately facilitating their placement in leading industries and organizations.

Industry Partnerships

Over the years, RIT has forged strong relationships with industries across the country, resulting in impressive placement records. Our training curriculum is aligned with industry requirements, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for entry-level positions. We also facilitate various industry-institute initiatives such as campus recruitment, industrial training, guest lectures, and alumni interactions.
